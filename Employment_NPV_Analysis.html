Data Assumptions, Sources, and Calculations:

  • Position information, education required, and median 2016 salaries from Bureau of Labor Statistics - Employment Projections
  • Undergraduate tuition cost from National Center for Education Statistics - Average Cost of Attenance
  • Graduate tuition cost from National Center for Education Statistics - Average Cost of Attenance
  • Doctoral and Professional Degree tuition cost from National Center for Education Statistics - Average Cost of Attenance
  • To adjust for data availabilty by year and salary increases, the 10 year average annual compensation change was from Bureau of Labor Statistics - Employment Cost Index
  • To adjust for data availabilty by year and tuition changes, the average tuition change was from National Center for Education Statistics - 5 Year Constant Dollar Change
  • Undergraduate and Graduate average loan repayments in years were from US Department of Education - Federal Student Aid Office
  • Undergraduate and Graduate average annual interest rates were from US Department of Education - Federal Student Aid Office
  • Discount rate used for net present value calculations was the 30 Year Treasury Rate from US Treasury - Daily Yield Curve Rates as of 3/27/18
  • Average medical and advanced dentistry residency years and salary were from MEDSCAPE - Resident Salary Report
  • Assumption: Only education costs and expected salary were considered. Living expenses, books, supplies, additional fees, etc. were not considered in this analysis
  • Assumption: Time series analysis was set at 45 years total, divided between applicable position education years and working years
  • Assumption: Education attendance was assumed to be full-time and all education costs were assumed to be borrowed and paid back based on average undergraduate and graduate repayment years
  • Assumption: Median career salary was expected at the midpoint of working years (median salary was escalated appropriately based on average compensataion change)
  • Calculation: Present Value of Annuity formula was used to determine total annual student loan payment after all education years (with tuition escalation)
  • Calculation: Net Present Value formula was used to net any annual student loan payments with projected annual salary (with salary escalation)
